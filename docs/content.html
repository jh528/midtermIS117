Latest paint trends of 2018

Working at Lowe's has been great so far. The top five paint trends of this year has been gray, greige, beige, off-white and blue. I find these colors often mixed for whole house themes in comparison to the majority of the other colors I have seen. Ideally, I would like to encourage more bold colors with accent walls and to consider painting front doors other than red and white.
